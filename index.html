About

I wear a lot of hats. During the day I'm a code jockey, working on everything from old vb6 desktop applications to full-stack, multi-tier applications and anything in between. I could be troubleshooting an issue with a Windows server, trying to get a build through Jenkins, deploying apps to OpenShift through Urban Code Deploy, or trying to figure out why a dockerfile is the way it is.

When my eyes cross and it's time to go home, I retreat to my family, where I try to be a supportive husband, dad, part-time youth lacrosse coach, and dog walker. Emphasis on the try part here, because like most of my professional career, I am learning how to wear all of these hats as I go. And everyone knows you don't learn anything if you don't make mistakes.

I'm glad you've decided to visit my website. Below you will find out a little more about my professional experience and background. Don't be a stranger!
